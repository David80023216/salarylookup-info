Overview: Product Manager Salary in Dallas

If you're researching the average Product Manager salary in Dallas, you've come to the right place. Based on comprehensive data gathered from thousands of job postings and employee reports across Dallas, the typical Product Manager earns approximately $135,000 per year in this market. This figure puts Dallas approximately average compared to the national average for this role.

The salary range for a Product Manager in Dallas typically spans from $100,000 at entry level to $170,000 for senior professionals with extensive experience. The exact figure you can expect depends on several variables including your years of experience, your educational background, the size of your employer, and the specific industry you work in.

What Factors Influence Product Manager Salaries in Dallas?

Understanding the factors that drive compensation helps you negotiate more effectively and plan your career path. Here are the primary variables that affect how much a Product Manager earns in Dallas:

  • Experience Level: Entry-level professionals typically start near $100,000, while those with 5–10 years of experience often reach the mid-range of $135,000. Senior-level roles at top companies can approach or exceed $170,000.
  • Education: A bachelor's degree is typically the minimum for most Product Manager positions. Advanced degrees or certifications can significantly boost earning potential by 15–25%.
  • Company Size: Large enterprises and Fortune 500 companies in Dallas generally pay more than small-to-medium businesses, often offering additional equity and bonus compensation.
  • Industry: Technology, finance, and healthcare sectors tend to offer the highest salaries for Product Manager roles in Dallas.
  • Cost of Living: Dallas's cost of living directly influences local wage rates. Employers in high-cost cities typically adjust compensation accordingly.
