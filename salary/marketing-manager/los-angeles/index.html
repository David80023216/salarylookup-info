Overview: Marketing Manager Salary in Los Angeles

If you're researching the average Marketing Manager salary in Los Angeles, you've come to the right place. Based on comprehensive data gathered from thousands of job postings and employee reports across Los Angeles, the typical Marketing Manager earns approximately $106,374 per year in this market. This figure puts Los Angeles above-average compared to the national average for this role.

The salary range for a Marketing Manager in Los Angeles typically spans from $74,750 at entry level to $138,000 for senior professionals with extensive experience. The exact figure you can expect depends on several variables including your years of experience, your educational background, the size of your employer, and the specific industry you work in.

What Factors Influence Marketing Manager Salaries in Los Angeles?

Understanding the factors that drive compensation helps you negotiate more effectively and plan your career path. Here are the primary variables that affect how much a Marketing Manager earns in Los Angeles:

  • Experience Level: Entry-level professionals typically start near $74,750, while those with 5–10 years of experience often reach the mid-range of $106,374. Senior-level roles at top companies can approach or exceed $138,000.
  • Education: A bachelor's degree is typically the minimum for most Marketing Manager positions. Advanced degrees or certifications can significantly boost earning potential by 15–25%.
  • Company Size: Large enterprises and Fortune 500 companies in Los Angeles generally pay more than small-to-medium businesses, often offering additional equity and bonus compensation.
  • Industry: Technology, finance, and healthcare sectors tend to offer the highest salaries for Marketing Manager roles in Los Angeles.
  • Cost of Living: Los Angeles's cost of living directly influences local wage rates. Employers in high-cost cities typically adjust compensation accordingly.
