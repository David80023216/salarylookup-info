Overview: Data Scientist Salary in San Antonio

If you're researching the average Data Scientist salary in San Antonio, you've come to the right place. What Factors Influence Data Scientist Salaries in San Antonio?

Understanding the factors that drive compensation helps you negotiate more effectively and plan your career path. Here are the primary variables that affect how much a Data Scientist earns in San Antonio:

  • Experience Level: Entry-level professionals typically start near $81,000, while those with 5–10 years of experience often reach the mid-range of $110,250. Senior-level roles at top companies can approach or exceed $139,500.
  • Education: A bachelor's degree is typically the minimum for most Data Scientist positions. Advanced degrees or certifications can significantly boost earning potential by 15–25%.
  • Company Size: Large enterprises and Fortune 500 companies in San Antonio generally pay more than small-to-medium businesses, often offering additional equity and bonus compensation.
  • Industry: Technology, finance, and healthcare sectors tend to offer the highest salaries for Data Scientist roles in San Antonio.
  • Cost of Living: San Antonio's cost of living directly influences local wage rates. Employers in high-cost cities typically adjust compensation accordingly.
