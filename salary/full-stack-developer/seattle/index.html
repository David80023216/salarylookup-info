Overview: Full Stack Developer Salary in Seattle

If you're researching the average Full Stack Developer salary in Seattle, you've come to the right place. Based on comprehensive data gathered from thousands of job postings and employee reports across Seattle, the typical Full Stack Developer earns approximately $143,750 per year in this market. This figure puts Seattle above-average compared to the national average for this role.

The salary range for a Full Stack Developer in Seattle typically spans from $106,250 at entry level to $181,250 for senior professionals with extensive experience. The exact figure you can expect depends on several variables including your years of experience, your educational background, the size of your employer, and the specific industry you work in.

What Factors Influence Full Stack Developer Salaries in Seattle?

Understanding the factors that drive compensation helps you negotiate more effectively and plan your career path. Here are the primary variables that affect how much a Full Stack Developer earns in Seattle:

  • Experience Level: Entry-level professionals typically start near $106,250, while those with 5–10 years of experience often reach the mid-range of $143,750. Senior-level roles at top companies can approach or exceed $181,250.
  • Education: A bachelor's degree is typically the minimum for most Full Stack Developer positions. Advanced degrees or certifications can significantly boost earning potential by 15–25%.
  • Company Size: Large enterprises and Fortune 500 companies in Seattle generally pay more than small-to-medium businesses, often offering additional equity and bonus compensation.
  • Industry: Technology, finance, and healthcare sectors tend to offer the highest salaries for Full Stack Developer roles in Seattle.
  • Cost of Living: Seattle's cost of living directly influences local wage rates. Employers in high-cost cities typically adjust compensation accordingly.
