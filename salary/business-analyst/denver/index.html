Overview: Business Analyst Salary in Denver

If you're researching the average Business Analyst salary in Denver, you've come to the right place. Based on comprehensive data gathered from thousands of job postings and employee reports across Denver, the typical Business Analyst earns approximately $99,750 per year in this market. This figure puts Denver approximately average compared to the national average for this role.

The salary range for a Business Analyst in Denver typically spans from $73,500 at entry level to $126,000 for senior professionals with extensive experience. What Factors Influence Business Analyst Salaries in Denver?

Understanding the factors that drive compensation helps you negotiate more effectively and plan your career path. Here are the primary variables that affect how much a Business Analyst earns in Denver:

  • Experience Level: Entry-level professionals typically start near $73,500, while those with 5–10 years of experience often reach the mid-range of $99,750. Senior-level roles at top companies can approach or exceed $126,000.
  • Education: A bachelor's degree is typically the minimum for most Business Analyst positions. Advanced degrees or certifications can significantly boost earning potential by 15–25%.
  • Company Size: Large enterprises and Fortune 500 companies in Denver generally pay more than small-to-medium businesses, often offering additional equity and bonus compensation.
  • Industry: Technology, finance, and healthcare sectors tend to offer the highest salaries for Business Analyst roles in Denver.
  • Cost of Living: Denver's cost of living directly influences local wage rates. Employers in high-cost cities typically adjust compensation accordingly.
