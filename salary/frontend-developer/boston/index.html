Overview: Frontend Developer Salary in Boston

If you're researching the average Frontend Developer salary in Boston, you've come to the right place. Based on comprehensive data gathered from thousands of job postings and employee reports across Boston, the typical Frontend Developer earns approximately $123,000 per year in this market. This figure puts Boston above-average compared to the national average for this role.

The salary range for a Frontend Developer in Boston typically spans from $90,000 at entry level to $156,000 for senior professionals with extensive experience. What Factors Influence Frontend Developer Salaries in Boston?

Understanding the factors that drive compensation helps you negotiate more effectively and plan your career path. Here are the primary variables that affect how much a Frontend Developer earns in Boston:

  • Experience Level: Entry-level professionals typically start near $90,000, while those with 5–10 years of experience often reach the mid-range of $123,000. Senior-level roles at top companies can approach or exceed $156,000.
  • Education: A bachelor's degree is typically the minimum for most Frontend Developer positions. Advanced degrees or certifications can significantly boost earning potential by 15–25%.
  • Company Size: Large enterprises and Fortune 500 companies in Boston generally pay more than small-to-medium businesses, often offering additional equity and bonus compensation.
  • Industry: Technology, finance, and healthcare sectors tend to offer the highest salaries for Frontend Developer roles in Boston.
  • Cost of Living: Boston's cost of living directly influences local wage rates. Employers in high-cost cities typically adjust compensation accordingly.
