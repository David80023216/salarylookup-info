About SalaryLookup.info

SalaryLookup.info is a free salary research tool designed to help job seekers, employees, and employers understand fair market compensation across the United States.

Our Mission

We believe that salary transparency empowers workers. When people know what they should be earning, they can negotiate better, make smarter career decisions, and close the pay gap. Our mission is to make accurate salary data freely accessible to everyone.

How We Collect Data

Our salary data is aggregated from multiple sources including publicly available job postings, government labor statistics from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), and industry surveys. We update our data regularly to reflect current market conditions.

We apply statistical adjustments for cost-of-living differences between cities to ensure our city-specific figures are as accurate as possible.

Who We Are

SalaryLookup.info is an independent salary research platform dedicated to providing unbiased, data-driven compensation information. We are not affiliated with any employer, recruiter, or staffing agency.

Disclaimer

Salary figures on this site represent estimates based on aggregated data and should be used as a general guide. Actual salaries vary based on individual qualifications, employer policies, and other factors. See our full Disclaimer for more information.
